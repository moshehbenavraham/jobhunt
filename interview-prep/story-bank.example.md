# Story Bank -- Master STAR+R Stories

Optional starter scaffold. Copy this to `interview-prep/story-bank.md` when you
want a reusable local story bank for interview prep.

This file accumulates your best interview stories over time. Each evaluation
(Block F) can add new stories here. Instead of memorizing 100 answers, maintain
5-10 deep stories that you can bend to answer almost any behavioral question.

## How it works

1. Every time `/jobhunt oferta` generates Block F (Interview Plan), new STAR+R
   stories can be appended here.
2. Before your next interview, review this file -- your stories are already
   organized by theme.
3. The "Big Three" questions can often be answered from this bank:
   - "Tell me about yourself" -> combine 2-3 stories into a narrative
   - "Tell me about your most impactful project" -> pick your highest-impact story
   - "Tell me about a conflict you resolved" -> find a story with a Reflection
